War Thunder

War Thunder is a free-to-play vehicular combat multiplayer video game developed and published by Gaijin Entertainment. The game is dedicated to World War II military aviation, armored vehicles, and naval vessels. It features combined arms battles on air, land, and sea with vehicles from the interwar period and the Cold War.

In War Thunder, players can choose from a wide variety of aircraft, tanks, and ships from different nations, each with its own unique characteristics and capabilities. The game offers several modes of play, including arcade battles, realistic battles, and simulator battles, catering to different playstyles and skill levels.

War Thunder has a large and active player base, with regular updates and expansions that introduce new vehicles, maps, and gameplay features. The game also includes a single-player mode with missions and campaigns for players who prefer to play solo.

Overall, War Thunder is known for its detailed vehicle models, realistic physics, and engaging gameplay, making it a popular choice among fans of military simulation games.
